+ [Pyserial](https://matplotlib.org/)
+ [Matplotlib](https://pyserial.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)
+ [Adafruit_IO](https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_IO_Python)

sudo pip3 install matplotlib pyserial adafruit_io
